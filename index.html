Our vision at Samrakhsha

Samrakhsha is not just about awareness — it is a smart platform connecting citizens and municipal authorities. Through our website, residents can report waste issues.We focus on effective waste management, plastic reduction, recycling awareness, and cleanliness initiatives. By combining technology, community participation, and governmental support, we aim to turn waste into resources and build sustainable habits that benefit everyone.

Did You Know?

  • The average person generates about 4.5 pounds of waste per day.
  • Recycling one ton of paper can save 17 trees and 7,000 gallons of water.
  • Composting organic waste can reduce landfill waste by up to 30%.
  • E-waste is the fastest-growing waste stream globally, with over 50 million tons generated annually.

Community Recycling

Recycling and environmental responsibility, emphasizing how individuals can work together to properly dispose of recyclable materials.

Waste Segregation

It visually categorizes waste into six distinct bins: Paper, Metal, Plastic, E-Waste, Glass, and Organic, each with specific colors and symbols.

Air Quality Alert

Highlights the dangers of air pollution and its harmful effects on human health. It aims to raise awareness about the negative impact of industrial emissions on air quality and encourage action to reduce pollution and protect public health.
